About Us Unleash Your Inner Beast

At Density Fitness, we are not only a positive vibe filled gym in the Redmond community, but we are also passionate in working with eachother to grow and learn throughout our fitness journey. Our goal is to help deepen every member's knowledge in fitness, whether if you're trying to build muscle, train as a performance athelete, or trying to get in shape. Our friendly staff is always willing to give tips and advice along the way whether you're a personal training client or not. The more dense your fitness knowledge the more you'll love working out with us.

Our Facilities

we aim to transform your abilities


Our 4,000sq/ft facility is filled with positive vibes and high energy that's open and available to members 24/7. We offer all the the basic and top of the line multifunctional equipment to help our clients reach their goals. The gym is undergoing a rebrand and redesigned with new colors and machines to empower every client to achieve more out of their work out. We are located on Avondale Road in the heart of the busiest streets right next to PCC and across the street from Starbucks. There is plenty of parking outside of the gym where members can easily walk a short distance into our facility. We are currently going through a moment of growth where more personal trainers are onboarding and new equipment are being replaced along the way to provide our members the best possible experience.

Trainers BUILDING A COMMUNITY OF athletes

Our trainers are skilled working with clientels in different niche and specialties. Our variety of trainers can accommodate everyone from beginner to advanced gym goers, weight loss, senior health and maintenance, and muscle building. We implement custom 1 on 1 training with monthly checkins to make sure our clients feel the results they want to experience. Each plan is uniquely yours and will never be a cookie cutter plan. As an additional FREE service to our clients working with our trainers you will have access to use our state of the art Inbody body composition measurement machine to help track your progress that is over 95% accurate than the dunk tank method.
